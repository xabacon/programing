Early Game (1–5 effects) — Zen / Simple Fun

Bleeding – Reduce total score by 1 for next 3 rolls per triggered die.
“The die draws blood! Each bleeding die saps your strength slightly.”

Freeze – Reduce dice sides by 1 for the next roll.
“Cold as ice! Chills your next roll.”

Critical Hit – Double the die’s value.
“Lucky strike! Smash for double points.”

Corruption – Adds 0.5–1% corruption per triggered die.
“Dark magic seeps in! Corruption spreads.”

Spark – Adds +1 score to the roll.
“Tiny burst of energy! Small but satisfying boost.”

Mid Game (6–10 effects) — More Interaction / Slight Chaos

Re-roll – Re-roll this die once.
“Fickle fate! Roll again for better or worse.”

Multiplier – Multiply this die by 1.5 (round down).
“Numbers grow! Small multiplier makes each roll matter.”

Decay – Reduce dice amount by 1 for next roll.
“Dice crumble… fewer chances next time.”

Inflate – Increase dice amount by 1 for next roll.
“Bonus dice appear! Luck smiles upon you.”

Swap – Swap this die’s value with another random die in the same roll.
“Dice swap! Could help… or hurt.”

Late Game (11–15 effects) — Chaos Mode

Explosion – Add 2–4 extra dice to this roll only.
“Boom! Extra dice spring into existence.”

Steal – Take 5% of total score from last roll.
“Thief in the night! Score siphoned temporarily.”

Mirror – Repeat this die’s value on the next roll.
“Your past echoes… sometimes good, sometimes bad.”

Invert – Subtract this die value from score instead of adding.
“Upside down! Positive becomes negative.”

Chain – Trigger one random effect from the set on another die in the same roll.
“One effect calls another… chaos spreads.”

Extra Late Game (16–20 effects) — Full Madness

Surge – Double the effect of a random late-game effect already triggered this roll.
“Power amplifies! Late-game chaos intensifies.”

Time Warp – Double score of this die, but skip its next roll.
“Fast forward… but pay the price later.”

Explosion+ – Add 5–10 dice to this roll only.
“Dice everywhere! Pure spectacle.”

Reverse Corruption – Reduces corruption by 1% per triggered die.
“A rare boon! Negative becomes slightly positive.”

Chaos Surge – Trigger 2–3 random effects on random dice in this roll.
“Absolute madness! Multiple effects collide.”
